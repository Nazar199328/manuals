Important Cheats
       
Once the console is activated , then now the following keys are to you at the disposal:

Key             Meaning

F1              Snapshots provide screen SHOT in the file
F2              between windowed mode and frame
F3              Screen representation/out
F5              QuickSave
F6              Time passes much faster
F7              Slow motion
F8              heal and a reviving after death
F9              Quickload


In addition, you can enter the following Cheats into the console, which must be confirmed with 
the ENTER key. EVERYTHING is CASE SENSITIVE.

Cheat                           Effect

god                             invulnerably
fullhealth                      Life energy maximize
spawn                           spawns a creature,monster, or NPC
spawn SH                        Mr. Help appears, which gives you with all kinds of cheats - But it may only work for German version -possibly
one have to type in "marvin" (without the quotes THEN open the console or something.) He used to be called the Character Helper (CH) in Gothic II.
idkfa                           Gives 100 points to Every SKILL (but does not activate it for you) and 100 of practically every item in the game- (could mess up quests but WOW)
teach                           teaches you specific thing - see the list
teach all                       teaches everything, but sets your stats to 0
give                            gives you a particular thing  in front of you (see list)
INSERT                          gives you a particular thing and places it in your inventory (see lists)
